Loaded Burrito Bowl

Forget Chipotle!, try this!

An easy to cook, delicious bowl with minimum time and ingredients needed.
With the hint of lemon and chillies, it will be a rollercoaster of flavors in your mouth

    Ingredients
  • 1 c. white rice
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 tsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 tbsp. chili seasoning
  • Ground black pepper
  • 1 c. shredded Monterey Jack
  • 1/4 c. freshly chopped cilantro
  • 1 c. Pico de Gallo
  • 1 c. guacamole
  • 8 oz. shredded cheddar
  • Tortilla chips, broken
  • 1 c. sour cream
      • Steps
  • In a pot, cook rice with 1/2 tsp. salt.
  • In a large skillet, add oil and cook ground breef until browned. Add chili seasoning, salt, black pepper, and some Monterey Jack
  • Finally in a bowl, add the cooked rice and beef, top it up with Pico de Gallo, guacamole, cheddar cheese, sour cream, and some tortilla chips.

    • Enjoy!
