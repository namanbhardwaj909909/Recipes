Quesadilla

A classic Mexican food that you can never go wrong with!

    Ingredients
  • Large flour tortillas
  • Grates cheddar cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Green onions
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Chicken
      • Steps
  • In a medium skillet, add some oil and heat the tortillas on it.
  • On another skillet, add oil and cook the chicken
  • Layer cheese on top of the tortilla as a base.
  • Add green onions, chopped tomatoes, and cooked chicken on it. Let everything settle down
  • Once the cheese is melted, fold the tortilla
  • Finally cut the tortilla into wedges and serve it with some salsa and sour cream

    • Enjoy!
